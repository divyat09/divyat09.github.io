I am a Research Fellow at Microsoft Research Lab India, where I work with Amit Sharma on projects related to Causal Inference and Explainable Machine Learning.
Prior to joining MSR, I completed my undergraduate double major program in Mathematics and Computer Science from the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur. There I worked with Prof. Piyush Rai and Prof. Vinay Namboodiri on Zero Shot Learning, Hierarchical Reinforcement Learning, and Program Synthesis. I was selected for the AScI internship program, where I joined the Probabilistic Machine Learning group under Prof. Samuel Kaski. I also interned at the National University of Singapore (Prof. Wynne Hsu, Prof. Lee Mong Li) and did a remote internship with the University of Texas at Dallas (Prof. Vincent Ng).
Interests:
Machine Learning; Causal Inference; Explainability, Generalization and Robustness in Deep Learning
